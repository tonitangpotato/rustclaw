Key design decisions:
- `JobMode::{Initial, ReExtract}` — ReExtract is non-droppable per §5.2 (operator-triggered, bypasses cap).

- `close()` for shutdown drain — even ReExtract is rejected when closed (shutdown wins over non-droppable).
