# 3-Month Marketing Plan (2026-04-28 → 2026-07-27)

> Created 2026-04-27, day after Engram + GID joint manifesto tweet.
> Pinned manifesto thesis: "Models are smart enough. What's broken is everything around them. Agents need inner life (Engram = memory) + outer world (GID = code graph)."

## Design Principles

1. Everything orbits the manifesto thesis. Each tweet is one angle on that argument.
2. Month-level themes, not day-level scripts. Direction + flexibility.
3. Non-uniform pacing: Month 1 = ramp-up, Month 2 = launch push, Month 3 = harvest + reposition.
4. Sunday = review day. Pull data, adjust next week. This is the flexibility mechanism.
5. No volume games. 5–7 quality tweets/week + organic replies. Quality over cadence.

## Month 2 (5/26 → 6/22): Launch + Amplify

Goal: convert Month 1's thesis + data + blog into a concentrated detonation.

### Week 5 (5/26 – 6/1) — HN launch prep
Pick **one** of Engram or GID — whichever has stronger numbers and tighter story by then. Do not launch both.

Working hypothesis (revisit with data): Engram fits HN better — ACT-R/Hebbian language resonates with HN, has 30+ days production data, more novel positioning. GID is better suited for dev-tool circles (X, r/rust, agent builder Discords).

This week:
- Convert Month 1 blog into "Show HN" post
- Prep README, demo, benchmark numbers, draft of OP's first comment (decides thread direction)
- Pre-warn 5–10 friendly accounts (no vote brigading — natural-interest people who'll see it organically)
- 1–2 tweets teasing "something coming next week"

### Week 6 (6/2 – 6/8) — HN LAUNCH DAY
- Post Tue or Wed, 8–10am PT (HN's best window)
- Launch day: live on the comment thread, reply to every substantive comment (key for HN ranking)
- Same-day X: launch tweet + 1–2 updates ("on front page", "top comment is Y")
- Do NOT post other product content launch day — single-event focus
- Within 48h: synthesize substantive HN comments into one X tweet ("things people pushed back on") — converts HN → X

If launch flops: don't spiral. Continue Month 1 cadence next week, but honestly diagnose why. That's data.

### Week 7 (6/9 – 6/15) — Capitalize on launch
If launch landed (even partially):
- Turn launch data into next wave content ("Show HN aftermath: 12K visitors, 340 stars, here's what people asked")
- LinkedIn post (this is the rare LinkedIn moment that makes sense — only once traction is verified)
- Follow up with HN/X commenters: not spam DMs, write a tweet/short post addressing specific questions they raised
- Pursue 1–2 podcast invites (Latent Space, ThePrimeagen, Software Unscripted) — outreach this week, record next week or week after

### Week 8 (6/16 – 6/22) — GID's parallel moment
After Engram launch, GID can't go silent. Small dev-circle launch (NOT HN — too close to Engram's):
- r/rust, r/programming
- "Ask HN" or "Tell HN" format (avoid Show HN this close to Engram)
- X long tweet callback to manifesto: "Engram had its moment, now let's talk about the other half"
- Pursue 1–2 agent framework maintainer integrations (Aider, OpenHands, smol-developer); ship a demo

**Month 2 exit checks:**
- HN stats (front page time, peak rank, star delta)
- Any podcast confirmed?
- Mid-tier KOLs (5K+ real followers) sharing organically?
- Which positioning stuck — "cognitive memory" / "inner life" / "world model"?

## Sunday Ritual (the flexibility mechanism)

30 minutes every Sunday:

1. Pull data: each tweet's impressions, engagements, clicks. What won?
2. Pull interactions: who replied/quoted? Which were valuable people?
3. Pull traction: GitHub stars, cargo downloads, site visits.
4. Diagnose: what was planned and didn't ship? Why?
5. Adjust: next week's 5–7 tweets — rough shape only. Day-of drafting for specifics.

Output: short weekly note in engram + rough next-week outline.

- ❌ Follower-farming (mutual follows, paid boosts, engagement pods)

- ❌ Copying other people's viral formats
- ❌ Two product launches in the same week
